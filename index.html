Welcome to Dojo Showdown!
Challenge the robots to a sparring match! Punches beat Kicks, Kicks beat Blocks, and Blocks beat Punches! Consecutive victories increase your score! If you lose, your score goes back to zero! Don't give up and try your best!

Controls:
Click around to change the camera angle
1-4: Select Opponent
A: Punch
W: Kick
D: Block
R: Reset
M: Toggle Music
